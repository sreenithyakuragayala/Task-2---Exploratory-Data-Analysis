Exploratory Data Analysis and Business Intelligence

Internship Task 2 - ApexPlanet Software Pvt. Ltd.

Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Business Intelligence on the Superstore dataset. The objective is to analyze sales data, identify patterns and trends, and generate actionable business insights through statistical analysis and data visualization.

Tools and Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- VS Code

Dataset

- Superstore Sales Dataset
- Total Records: 9,800
- Total Columns: 18

Project Tasks

1. Data Loading and Inspection
2. Data Cleaning and Quality Assessment
3. Descriptive Statistics
4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
5. Business Question Analysis
6. Data Visualization
7. Business Intelligence Reporting

Business Questions Addressed

- Total Sales by Category
- Total Sales by Month
- Total Sales by Region
- Total Sales by Customer Segment
- Top 10 Cities by Sales

Visualizations Created

- Sales Distribution Histogram
- Sales by Category Bar Chart
- Sales Share by Category Pie Chart
- Top 10 Cities by Sales
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment

Key Insights

- Identified category-wise sales performance.
- Analyzed monthly sales trends.
- Compared sales across different regions.
- Evaluated customer segment contributions.
- Identified top-performing cities based on sales.

Learning Outcomes

- Data preprocessing and cleaning techniques.
- Descriptive statistical analysis.
- Exploratory Data Analysis using Python.
- Data visualization using Matplotlib and Seaborn.
- Business intelligence and decision-support analysis.
